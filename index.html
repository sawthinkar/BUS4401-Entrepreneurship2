About the Founder

I am Saw Thinkar, an entrepreneur with a deep connection to the Karen community. Growing up in the Karen State of Myanmar, I witnessed firsthand the financial challenges faced by my people. My mission is to provide accessible and culturally sensitive banking solutions.
